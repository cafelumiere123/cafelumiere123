About Us

Welcome to Café de Lumière, where passion for great coffee meets a love for community and culture. Located in the heart of Valenzuela City, our café offers more than just a place to enjoy a cup of coffee — it’s a haven where friends gather, ideas are sparked, and moments are savored.
At Café de Lumière, we believe that coffee is an art form, and every cup we serve reflects our dedication to quality and craftsmanship. From rich, aromatic espresso to our selection of hand-crafted pastries, we strive to create an unforgettable experience for each of our guests. Whether you're starting your day with a fresh brew or unwinding with friends in the evening, our space is designed to be your perfect escape.


Our Story

Café de Lumière was founded by a group of friends who share a deep passion for coffee, food, and creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Our team — Candice Beran, Agilluz Bernardo, Marc Binay, and Daniel Castañeda — came together with a vision to bring a little light (or "lumière") to the café scene. With diverse backgrounds but a shared love for hospitality, we wanted to create a space that blends modern café culture with a touch of timeless charm.


Our Team

Candice Beran: A creative visionary, Candice brings her love for design and culture to every corner of Café de Lumière. She ensures that every detail, from décor to menu, creates a unique and cozy environment.
Aguilluz Bernardo: With a keen eye for quality and a passion for perfection, Aguilluz is the expert behind our signature blends and expertly crafted coffees.
Marc Binay: Our kitchen master, Marc oversees the culinary creations that accompany your coffee, ensuring every pastry and dish is made with care and love.
Daniel Castañeda: The heart of Café de Lumière, Daniel brings together the team’s vision and ensures every guest feels the warmth of our hospitality.


Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to create a café experience that offers not just exceptional food and drink, but also a space where people can connect, relax, and recharge. We’re more than just a coffee shop — we’re a community hub where light and life come together. Come join us at Café de Lumière and let us brighten your day with a perfect cup of coffee and a warm smile. We can’t wait to welcome you!
